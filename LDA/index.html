LDA Topic Analysis

Interactive visualization of topics in SemEval 2017 Rumor Stance dataset

About this visualization

These interactive visualizations use Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) to discover hidden topics in tweets. Each circle represents a topic, and you can click on them to see the most relevant words. The bars on the right show word frequencies within and across topics.

Stance Topics (S/D/Q)

Topics discovered in tweets that express a stance: Support, Deny, or Query towards rumor claims.

→

Comment Topics

Topics discovered in neutral comment tweets that neither support, deny, nor query the rumor.
